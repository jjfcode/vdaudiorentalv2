M80-WH

M80-WH Wireless Capsule

The M80-WH is the wireless capsule head variant of the M80 Dynamic. The wireless capsule is sonically identical to the M80 standard and M80-SH and has the same condenser like performance, minimal proximity effect, and superior feedback rejection one expects in its sibling products.

M81-WH

M81-WH Wireless Capsule

The M81-WH is the wireless capsule head variant of the M81 Univeral Dynamic. The wireless capsule is sonically identical to the M81 Standard and M81-SH and has the same condenser like performance, minimal proximity effect, and superior feedback rejection one expects in TELEFUNKEN Dynamic Series microphones. The M81 Universal Dynamic retains articulate mid-range as the M80 Dynamic, however, the top end is pulled back a bit yielding a flatter overall frequency response.
