ONYX 1640

16-Channel Compact Mixer

The Mackie Onyx 1640 is a premium 16-channel/four-bus small-format analog mixer. It has six aux sends with pre/post and solo switches. This mixer has four stereo line-level inputs. It has a built-in Talkback section that is suitable for internal or external mics. This mixer also offers selectable instrument inputs on first two channels. In addition, it features "Planet-Earth" switching power supply for universal use.
