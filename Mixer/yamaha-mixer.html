CL5

Digital Mixing Console

With a three-section fader layout for efficient hands-on control, the CL5 is the ideal choice for a diverse spectrum of live sound systems. Input channels: 72 mono, 8 stereo. Fader configuration: 16-fader left section, 8-fader Centralogic section, 8-fader right section, 2-fader master section . Stainless steel stay for iPad support. Built-in meter bridge.

QL1

Digital Mixing Console

16 + 2 fader configuration in a compact, rack mountable unit. Mix channels: 32 mono, 8 stereo. Busses: 16 mix, 8 matrix (Input to Matrix supported). Local I/O: 16 in, 8 out. Fader configuration: 16 + 2 (Master). Rack mountable with optional RK1 Rack Mount Kit.

Rio3224-D2

Rio3224-D2 I/O Rack

This high-capacity, high-performance I/O rack is compatible with Yamaha CL and QL series consoles as well as the RIVAGE PM series. It features 32 analog inputs, 16 analog outputs, and eight digital AES/EBU outputs. The Rio3224-D2 connects directly to Dante digital audio networks, allowing flexible system configuration. Dual power supply units are built in for high reliability, and a character/graphic display offers easy visual confirmation.
