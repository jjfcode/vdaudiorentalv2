P10T

Wireless Transmitter

Shure’s P10T is a networkable, full-rack, dual-channel wireless transmitter used in conjunction with the P10R Wireless Bodypack Receiver as part of the PSM® 1000 Personal Monitor System. The P10T Wireless Transmitter combines state-of-the-art technology with touring-grade features for professional monitoring applications of any size with no room for error. It offers a 72 MHz to 80 MHz tuning bandwidth, networked remote control via Wireless Workbench® software.

P9T

Wireless Transmitter

Shure’s P9T is a half-rack, wireless transmitter used in conjunction with the P9RA+ Bodypack receiver as part of the PSM® 900 Wireless Personal Monitor System.

P10R

Diversity Wireless Bodypack Receiver

Diversity wireless bodypack receiver offers premium features for use with the PSM 1000 In-Ear Personal Monitoring System. Includes RF Scan, twin-antenna diversity stereo encoding, CueMode for monitoring different mixes, and MixMode Technology.

P10R+

Diversity Wireless Bodypack Receiver

With a hybrid analog/digital architecture, the P10R+ twin-antenna diversity Bodypack Receiver offers the highest level of sound personalization yet, putting category-leading audio quality, robust RF performance, and touring-grade features right at your fingertips.

P9R+

Bodypack Receiver

Shure’s P9RA+ is a compact, stereo bodypack receiver for use with the PSM ®900 Wireless Personal Monitor System. With a hybrid analog/digital architecture, the P9RA+ Bodypack Receiver offers unparalleled audio quality with more headroom, better stereo separation, and higher fidelity, along with reliable RF performance for increased RF signal strength to meet the needs of professional audio applications of all sizes, including IEM and IFB applications.

P9HW

PSM 900 Wired Bodypack Personal Monitor

Wired bodypack personal monitor delivers improved audio clarity and reduced feedback onstage compared to traditional speaker wedges. Features include LCD screen, MixMode technology, and stereo mode for left or right balance adjustment for two mixes.

SB900A

Rechargeable Lithium-Ion Battery

Rechargeable lithium-ion battery makes powering Shure wireless systems easy. Features include compatibility with Axient Digital (AD1/AD2), ULX-D, QLX-D, UR5, P3RA, P9R, and P10R systems.

SBC-AX

Charging module

Charging module charges 2 SB900A Rechargeable Batteries in the SBRC Rack Mount Charging Station.

SBRC

Shure Battery Rack Charger

Accommodates up to 8 Shure rechargeable bodypack and handheld transmitter batteries in a single rack space. Compatible with Shure rechargeable batteries SB910, SB920, SB910M, AXT910, AXT920 and SB900A. Charging modules sold separately.

SBC800

8-Up Battery Charger

8-battery charger charges 8 SB900A batteries to full capacity within 3 hours. Features include status LEDs to indicate power levels.

PA805

Directional Antenna

Directional antenna for increased range and reduced interference as compared to omnidirectional 1/2-wave antennas.

PA821B

Antenna Combiner for PSM® Wireless Systems

Eight-to-One Wideband PSM Antenna Combiner for all P3T, P9T and P10T Transmitters (470-865 MHz)

PA421B
