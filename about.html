About

VD Audio Rental was founded in 2009. Our vision began with the desire to become a trusted resource for live production and concert touring companies. We have since provided support for a variety of production events locally and abroad. With years of experience under our belt, we strive to be the first source for audio rental solutions. It is our priority to build relationships with our clients. Along with having a large inventory of professional audio gear available, we are proud to have a dedicated team with real-world knowledge in live production. This enables us to provide personal support for quick solutions. VD Audio Rental continues to evolve and expand to benefit our client's needs.
